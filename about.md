# About

<em>DELUS</em> began in 2021, initiated by former LUS doctoral fellows Sara Frikech and Johanna Just as a platform to engage with emerging questions in landscape and urban studies. Emerging from the Institute for Landscape and Urban Studies (LUS) at ETH Zürich, <em>DELUS</em> is a research-driven journal fostering dialogue across disciplines and institutions. Situated at the intersection of architecture, landscape, and urban studies, it gives visibility to new topics, concepts, and perspectives shaping contemporary discourse.
